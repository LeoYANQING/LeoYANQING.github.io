Research project at Soochow University (2020.09 - 2021.06), supervised by Prof. Lu Bo.

Designed a continuum robot platform for colonoscopy assistance:
- Upgraded from 4-DOF to 6-motor design for higher precision
- Implemented A* path planning algorithm on STM32 microcontroller
- Calibrated angle and motion control for multi-segment bending
- Analyzed error propagation across joint segments and bend complexity levels
