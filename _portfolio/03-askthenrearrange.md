Research at Tongji University CDI (2025.12 - 2026.03), supervised by Prof. Guo Weiwei.

[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/LeoYANQING/AskThenRearrange)

- Designed robot questioning strategy based on KCQ encoding from real human dialogue patterns
- Conducted 24-group 1v1 real user experiments: result-oriented vs. process-oriented tasks
- Found that process-oriented tasks (e.g., tidying) are better suited for user-preference questioning strategy
- Deployed on Unitree humanoid robot
- **Paper**: *Task Matters: Investigating Human Questioning Behavior in Different Household Service for Learning by Asking Robots* (in progress)
