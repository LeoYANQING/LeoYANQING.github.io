Research project at Soochow University (2023.03 - 2023.05).

Applied Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) and 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) to endoscopic surgical scenes:
- **Instrument removal**: removing surgical instruments from scene rendering (defibrillator surgery)
- **Tissue reconstruction**: 3D reconstruction of abdominal cavity tissue from laparoscopic video
- **Surgical measurement**: distance measurement in reconstructed 3D surgical scenes

Challenges addressed: noisy endoscopic images, difficult camera pose estimation, and motion blur in natural cavities.
